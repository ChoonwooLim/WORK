Frequently Asked Questions

How am I billed? ▼
Workspace plan costs and used compute resources are billed tracking. Compute is pro-rated per second, so if you use it for one day you only pay for one day’s usage.
Am I charged in months with no activity? ▼
Member fees are waived in months with no services (live or paused) and no activity in the workspace.
What are the Free plan limitations? ▼
Starter plan is limited to 3 projects, 100GB/mo bandwidth, and 1 user. Designed for personal projects and learning, AI error analysis and GPU streaming are available on Pro & Up plans.
What payment methods are supported? ▼
All major credit and debit cards are supported. Payment info is securely processed by Stripe and not stored on our servers.
Can I change my plan anytime? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime in the dashboard. Upgrades apply immediately, downgrades apply at the end of the current billing cycle.
How is Unreal Pixel Streaming billed? ▼
Billed per second based on GPU instance usage time. Dual GPU Load Balancing supports Team and up, single GPU streaming is available from Pro plan.
I want to know more about the Enterprise plan. ▼
Enterprise plan offers custom pricing tailored to your org’s needs. Features Dedicated GPU Clusters, SLA Guaranteed Uptime, Dedicated Customer Success Manager. Contact contact@twinverse.orgfor detailed consultation.
